% Test basic features of formulas

\starttext
Simple formula
\startformula
  E = mc^2
\stopformula

Formula with an equation number
\placeformula \startformula
  E = mc^2
\stopformula

Formula with an referenced number
\placeformula[energy] \startformula
  E = mc^2
\stopformula
Einstein gave the famous formula~(\in[energy]).

No formula number
\placeformula[-] \startformula
  E = mc^2
\stopformula

\stoptext
